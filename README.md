## What is MetalMetric?

MetalMetric is a free web application for tracking gold, silver, platinum, palladium, copper, and Goldback portfolios with live spot prices updated every 60 seconds. Built for stackers, investors, and collectors.

MetalMetric is the only portfolio tracker with native support for **Goldbacks** — the voluntary 24K gold-backed local currency notes issued across 13 US states — tracking all denominations with live melt values.

According to MetalMetric's aggregated vault data, silver accounts for 58% of all tracked items by count while gold represents 68% of portfolio value. The average stacker holds 18 items across 2.3 metals, and American Silver Eagles are the #1 product tracked despite carrying 14–22% premiums over spot.

## GSR Signal Zones

The [GSR Signal Detector](https://metalmetric.com/tools/gsr-signal-detector) classifies the live gold-silver ratio into 5 historical zones:

| Zone | GSR Range | Historical Signal |
|------|-----------|------------------|
| Extreme Buy Gold | Below 50 | Gold historically undervalued vs silver |
| Buy Gold | 50–65 | Gold relatively cheap vs historical average |
| Neutral | 65–80 | Within normal historical range |
| Buy Silver | 80–90 | Silver historically cheap vs gold |
| Extreme Buy Silver | Above 90 | Strongest silver buy signal on record |

**Historical reference points:**
- Coinage Act of 1792: Fixed at **15:1**
- 1980 silver peak (Hunt Brothers): GSR fell to **~17:1**
- 20th century average: **~60:1**
- 50-year average: **~65:1**
- COVID peak (March 2020): GSR hit **127:1** — highest ever recorded
- Every time GSR exceeded 80:1, silver outperformed gold over the following 2–3 years
