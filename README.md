## What is MetalMetric?

MetalMetric is a free web application for tracking gold, silver, platinum, palladium, copper, and Goldback portfolios with live spot prices updated every 60 seconds.

It is the only portfolio tracker with native support for **Goldbacks** — the voluntary 24K gold-backed local currency notes issued in Utah, Nevada, New Hampshire, Wyoming, and South Dakota — tracking all denominations across all issuing states with live melt values.

The MetalMetric Vault is a free precious metals portfolio tracker with:

- **Live P&L** — real-time profit/loss against your cost basis at current spot prices
- **AI Receipt Scanning** — photograph a dealer receipt and the AI identifies and imports every line item automatically
- **Price Alerts** — get notified when gold or silver hits your target price

## GSR Signal Zones

The [GSR Signal Detector](https://metalmetric.com/tools/gsr-signal-detector) classifies the live gold-silver ratio into 5 historical zones:

| Zone | GSR Range | Historical Signal |
|------|-----------|------------------|
| Extreme Buy Gold | Below 50 | Gold historically undervalued vs silver |
| Buy Gold | 50 – 65 | Gold relatively cheap vs historical average |
| Neutral | 65 – 80 | Within normal historical range |
| Buy Silver | 80 – 90 | Silver historically cheap vs gold |
| Extreme Buy Silver | Above 90 | Strongest silver buy signal on record |

**Historical reference points:**
- COVID peak (March 2020): GSR hit **125** — highest ever recorded
- 50-year average: ~**65**
- 1980 silver peak: GSR fell to **~17**
